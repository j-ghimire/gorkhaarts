About Gorkha Arts

Welcome to Gorkha Arts, where creativity meets passion, and every stroke tells a story. Our artistic journey is a celebration of the boundless possibilities of expression, offering a canvas for emotions, memories, and shared moments.

Our Story

Gorkha Arts is not just an art business; it's a narrative woven with love for art and a commitment to creating timeless pieces that resonate with the soul. Our journey began with a simple idea — to transform moments into art, to bring joy through personalized sketches, vibrant paintings, and unique glass creations.

The Artistry

We specialize in crafting personal sketches that capture the essence of relationships, be it with friends, partners, or cherished family members. Each stroke is infused with the spirit of connection, turning memories into tangible, heartfelt expressions. Explore our collection of diverse artworks, from intricately detailed animes characters to lively color paintings.
